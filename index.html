01 / About Us

Built by law enforcement, for law enforcement.

Intellect LE was founded in 2024 with a clear mission — to provide quality training and affordable resources to the law enforcement community.

Specializing in ICAC and Sex Offender Supervision, our Sex Offender Management Symposium has trained hundreds of officers across the United States and internationally.

Through our travels, we've met incredible officers dedicated to keeping their communities safe, often with limited budgets and resources. That challenge inspired us to create accessible, cost-effective software and platforms that enhance investigative efficiency and documentation.

Intellect LE was built by active law enforcement professionals with extensive experience in ICAC investigations, sex offender supervision, K9 handling, and general investigations. Our goal is simple — to support agencies of all sizes, from rural departments to large metropolitan task forces, with tools that strengthen their mission regardless of budget.
