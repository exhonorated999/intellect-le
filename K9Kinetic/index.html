Why We Built It

A thank-you to K9 teams nationwide.

K9 Kinetic was created by a former K9 handler who lived the frustration firsthand — scrambling to piece together monthly logs, chasing scattered documentation, and dreading the paperwork that came after every deployment.

The mission was simple: build the tool we always wished we had. Something handlers actually look forward to opening — fast, organized, and built around how K9 work really happens in the field.

It's designed to support both civilian and law enforcement K9 teams, because every team deserves documentation that protects them and celebrates the work they do.

Key Features

Everything a K9 unit needs, in one place.

Documentation

Training & Deployment Documentation

Capture the full picture of every shift and every session with fast, structured entries built for handlers.

  • Track deployments with detailed, timestamped notes
  • Log training sessions by focus area and time spent
  • Store annual certifications and keep them current
  • Maintain complete handler and K9 team history
Operational Tools

Field tools that ride along

The resource tray puts navigation and intelligence right next to your records — no juggling apps in the field.

  • Quick access to directions and turn-by-turn navigation
  • LPR data integration (Flock Safety, Vigilant)
  • Watch Duty open-source intelligence
  • Broadcastify scanner access
  • Training site & location tracking
Reporting

Organized, defensible reporting

Turn field activity into clean records supervisors and prosecutors can rely on — without the monthly scramble.

  • Type and store reports locally with rich text and photos
  • Organized, defensible documentation by case
  • Effortless monthly log management
Security & Accessibility

Your data stays yours

K9 Kinetic runs entirely on your machine — no accounts, no cloud, nothing to breach.
